COMPUTER ENGINEERING (CE) PROGRAM GUIDE (Fall 2024-Winter 2025)

DECLARATION REQUIREMENTS:
1. Completed at least one full term or 12 total credits at U of M Ann Arbor
2. Overall UM GPA of 2.0 or better
3. Completed courses with grade C or better:
   a. Calculus (e.g. Math 115, 116 or 156)
   b. Calculus-based physics lectures (e.g. Physics 140 or 160) or chemistry lectures (e.g. Chem 130)
   c. Required engineering courses (Engr 100, 101, or 151)

PROGRAM REQUIREMENTS:
1. College of Engineering Core Requirements (39 credits):
   - CHEM 125/126/130 or CHEM 210/211
   - ENGR 100, 101 (or 151 or EECS 180 AP credit or EECS 183)
   - MATH 115, 116, 214 (or 217 or 417 or 419), 216
   - PHYSICS 140/141, 240/241
   - Intellectual Breadth (16 credits)
   - General Electives (13 credits)

2. CE Program Core Courses (32 credits):
   - EECS 203, 215, 216, 270, 280, 370
   - EECS 301 or MATH 425 or STATS 412
   - EECS 496 or ENGR 499-002
   - TCHNCLCM 300, 496

3. Core Electives (8 credits):
   Two courses from: EECS 281, 312, 351, 373

4. Upper Level CE Electives (10 credits):
   At least 10 credits from approved list, including one Major Design Experience (MDE) course

5. EECS Elective (3 credits)

6. Flexible Technical Electives (7 credits)

MAJOR GRADING & REPEAT POLICIES:
- C or better required in all courses used toward CE requirements
- 200-level courses (EECS 203, 280, 281) limited to two attempts
