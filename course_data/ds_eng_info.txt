DATA SCIENCE PROGRAM - ENGINEERING (DS-ENG)
Fall 2024-Winter 2025 Program Guide

DECLARATION REQUIREMENTS:
1. Completed at least one full term or 12 total credits at UM Ann Arbor
2. Overall UM GPA of 2.0 or better
3. Completed courses with grade C or better:
   a. Calculus (e.g. Math 115, 116 or 156)
   b. Calculus-based physics lectures (e.g. Physics 140 or 160) or chemistry lectures (e.g. Chem 130)
   c. Required engineering courses (Engr 100, 101, or 151)

PROGRAM REQUIREMENTS:
1. College of Engineering Core Requirements (39 credits):
   - CHEM 125/126/130 or CHEM 210/211
   - ENGR 100, 101 (or 151 or EECS 180 AP credit or EECS 183 or Robotics 102)
   - MATH 115, 116, 214 (or 217 or 417), 215
   - PHYSICS 140/141, 240/241
   - Intellectual Breadth (16 credits)
   - General Electives (11-12 credits)

2. DS-Eng Program Core (34 credits):
   - Introductory Data Science: DATASCI 101 (strongly recommended), STATS 250, STATS 280, IOE 265, or ECON 451
   - EECS 203 or MATH 465: Discrete Mathematics
   - EECS 280: Programming and Elementary Data Structures
   - EECS 281: Data Structures and Algorithms
   - STATS 412 or MATH/STATS 425 or MATH/STATS 426: Introduction to Probability and Statistics
   - STATS/DATASCI 413: Applied Regression Analysis
   - Databases and Applications: EECS 484 or EECS 485
   - Machine Learning/Data Mining: EECS 445 or DATASCI 415
   - Data Science Applications elective (see online list)

3. Advanced Electives and Capstone (12 credits):
   - Advanced Technical Elective (4 credits)
   - Advanced Statistical Analysis Elective (4 credits)
   - Capstone Experience Course (4 credits)

4. Other Requirements:
   - Flexible Technical Electives (11 credits)
   - TCHNCLCM 300 (1 credit)
   - EECS 496 or ENGR 499-002 or approved alternative (2 credits)
   - TCHNCLCM 497 or STATS 404 or STATS 485 (2 credits)

MAJOR GRADING & REPEAT POLICIES:
- C or better required in all courses used to satisfy major requirements
- 200-level courses (EECS 203, 280, 281) limited to two attempts
